Empowering Future Leaders: The Consulting Championship 2025

The Consulting Championship has evolved into a flagship initiative to unlock the potential of Saudi Arabia's next generation of consultants.

    Who can participate

  • The championship is open to undergraduate students from one of the following universities.
    • Imam Abdulrahman Bin Faisal University (IAU)
    • Imam Mohammad Ibn Saud Islamic University (IMSIU)
    • King Abdulaziz University (KAU)
    • King Fahd University of Petroleum & Minerals (KFUPM)
    • King Faisal University (KFU)
    • King Khalid University (KKU)
    • King Saud University (KSU)
    • Prince Mohammad Bin Fahd University (PMU)
    • Princess Nourah bint Abdulrahman University (PNU)
    • Umm Al-Qura University (UQU)
  • All students must register in teams of three or four participants(one team leader).
  • Only the team leader submit the appliction.
  • All majors can apply.

Register in four steps

Deadline : Sep 10, 2025

Open the portal

Click “Register Now” to open the registration site.

Create your account

Only the team leader creates an account and verifies their email.

Complete one team form

Provide member details: full name, personal & university emails (.edu.sa), University ID, GPA & major, graduation year, and mobile phone (+966).

Get confirmation

Receive a confirmation email once the team form is submitted.

Championship Timeline

  • Registration Period
    Create your team (3-4 students) and complete the team form.
    Aug 24 - Sep 10
  • University visits
    On-campus briefings and Q&A with partners.
    Aug 25 - Sep 09
  • Consulting Fundamentals Program
    Workshops kick off; teams start preparing the case.
    Sep 16
  • Case Submission
    Upload your deck to the portal beforfe the deadline.
    Oct 14
  • Top 50 Presentations
    Shortlisted teams present to a judging panel.
    Oct 28 - Oct 30
  • Top 20 and one-to-one mentorship
    Deep-dive coaching with partners to refine your story.
    Nov 2 - Nov 25
  • Grand Finale
    Final presentations and awards ceremony.
    Nov 26 - Nov 27
